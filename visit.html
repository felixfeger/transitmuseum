Getting here

Take any City Metro line to Death Star City Station, then follow signs to the Rolling Stock Hall entrance.

Accessibility

The Rolling Stock Hall is step-free throughout. Wheelchairs are available at the front desk on request.

Admission

See the tickets page for current pricing and to get a ticket ahead of your visit.

Groups & schools

Group visits and school tours can be arranged in advance. Contact the museum office for details.
