Our story

The City Metro Transit Museum preserves the rolling stock, signage, and stories behind Lego City County's public transit system — from the earliest streetcars to today's rail network.

Mission

To collect, restore, and interpret the vehicles and artifacts that shaped how Lego City County moves, and to keep that history accessible to riders of every generation.

Collection

Our Rolling Stock Hall holds streetcars, shuttle cars, and subway cars retired from active City Metro service, each preserved in as close to original condition as possible.

Operated by

The museum is a project of the Republic States Railway and Transportation Authority, in partnership with City Metro and volunteer rail historians across the county.

Support us

Museum admission and special-event tickets directly fund restoration work. See our tickets page to help support the collection.
